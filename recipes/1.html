Schweinshaxe (Roasted Pork Knuckle)

Schweinshaxe is a traditional Bavarian dish that features a roasted pork knuckle. It is a popular delicacy during Oktoberfest and is known for its crispy skin and tender, flavorful meat. The dish is often served with sauerkraut and mashed potatoes, creating a hearty and satisfying meal.

Ingredients:

1 pork knuckle 1 kg
Salt 2 tsp
Caraway seeds 2 tsp
Ground black pepper 1 tsp
Garlic cloves, minced 3
Vegetable oil 2 tbsp

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Score the pork knuckle with a sharp knife.
  3. Rub salt, caraway seeds, black pepper, and minced garlic all over the pork knuckle.
  4. Heat vegetable oil in a large oven-safe skillet over medium heat.
  5. Place the pork knuckle in the skillet and sear until golden brown on all sides.
  6. Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and roast forabout 2-3 hours or until the pork knuckle is tender and the skin is crispy.
  7. Remove from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes before serving.
